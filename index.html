About Me

I’ve cared about sustainability for as long as I can remember. Growing up in Arizona and California, I lived through the effects of droughts and wildfires firsthand. My childhood taught me the importance of conservation and environmental responsibility.

That foundation led me to explore the challenges women face in climate-vulnerable regions—and later, how our digital consumption contributes to pollution in ways that remains largely invisible.

As a Computer Science major with a passion for marketing, I’m focused on using technology and storytelling to raise awareness and inspire change for the next generation. Whether I’m building a brand or working with data, I focus on creating with purpose—to connect with others, share insights, and support work that makes a real impact.
